Meet the baker

Every recipe carries her name

Deecrown Kitchen And Confectionery started in a home kitchen, with one mixing bowl and a lot of patience. Today it's a trusted name for celebration cakes, pastries and small chops across Ondo, built one order, and one happy customer, at a time.

Beyond baking, she teaches, running hands-on classes for beginners and aspiring bakers who want to learn the craft properly, from batter to buttercream.

Frequently asked questions

How do I place an order?

Send a message through the order form below or reach out directly on WhatsApp with your preferred flavour, size and date needed.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

At least 3–5 days for standard cakes, and 2–3 weeks for wedding or multi-tier celebration cakes to allow time for design and testing.

Do you deliver, and what areas do you cover?

Yes — delivery is available across Ondo, with a small fee based on distance. Pickup is also available from the kitchen.

Can I request a custom design or flavour?

Absolutely — most cakes are made to order. Share your theme, colours or flavour preferences and it'll be built around your request.

How do the baking classes work?

Classes run in small hands-on groups at the kitchen. Each session covers a specific skill, with all ingredients and equipment provided.

Do you offer private or one-on-one sessions?

Yes, private sessions can be arranged for individuals or small groups — just mention it when booking a class.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer is preferred, with a deposit required to confirm custom cake and class bookings.
